FOOT ANGLE

FOOT ANGLE is the angle between the shoe and the ground surface at landing. ORPHE CORE calculates the angle change between IC (Initial contact) and LR (Loading responce) as the foot angle. If you want to improve your running form, it’s important to pay attention to how your feet land on the ground as you run. More information on this topic can be found at https://media.orphe.io/keyword/landing.
