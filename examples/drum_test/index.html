ORPHE CORE Gesture Drum

This interactive application lets you play drum sounds using two ORPHE CORE devices and your own gestures.
The app detects acceleration and Euler angles to trigger various sounds and visualizations, creating a unique musical experience.
[How gestures trigger sounds]
- Swing your feet or hands in the "forward/backward", "left/right", or "up/down" directions. The app analyzes the acceleration and angles (pitch, roll, yaw) to play different drum sounds:
 - Large movement on the X axis (forward/backward): Depending on the pitch angle, different sounds (e.g., snare, high tom) are played.
 - Large movement on the Y axis (left/right): Depending on the roll angle, different sounds (e.g., hi-hat, crash) are played.
 - Large movement on the Z axis (up/down): Depending on the yaw angle, different sounds (e.g., bass drum, floor tom) are played.
- The sound changes according to the timing and angle of your gesture.
Connect your devices as instructed on the screen and enjoy making rhythms with your movements!
